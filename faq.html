Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Scholars' Bridge?

    Scholars' Bridge is an initiative that aims to bridge the gap that has been created by lack of curriculum review and help prepare individuals with skills and knowledge that will enable them add value to the society.

  • How do I get started?

    simply complete the sign up form in less than 2mins

    Step 1: Visit the Sign Up Page

    Step 2: Enter your Full Name, Email, and Password

    Step 4: Click Sign Up to Let's Go

  • What next after registering

    Click on Log In, Go to your dashboard and update your bio-data

    Boom! You are good to go!

  • Can I join without registering?

    partially! you can only view people's comments and rections but you can't contribute nor get access to the wealth of resouces here.
